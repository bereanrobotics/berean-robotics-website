Welcome to
Berean Christian Robotics

Our Berean Robotics Team consists of 21 members. We work together, in seperate squads, such as managment and builders, to achieve the major goal of succeeding in tournaments. But, at said tournaments, we make sure to put "Gracious Professionalism" above all else. We love to help others when they need it and to give our own time to support the efforts of other teams in order to make it a worthwhile experience for all participants.


The Berean Robotics Team is participating in FTC, the FIRST Tech Challenge. In the FIRST Tech Challenge, the teams are challenged to build, design, and program robots to complete tasks and score points. The competition for this year is First Relic Recovery, and the teams compete in an alliance format to score as many points as possibly by putting glyphs in their proper places, knocking jewels off platforms, and grabbing relics and relocating them outside the arena.
